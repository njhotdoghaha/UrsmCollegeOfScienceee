Vice President for Administration and Finance

Dr. Marvin P. Amoin

Vice President for Administration and Finance

The Vice-President for Administration and Finance is directly responsible to the University President on all administrative operations, planning, and finance matters. In addition, he/she shall perform the following specific duties and functions:

  • Exercise supervision over the administrative, finance, and general services of the University;
  • Exercise leadership and promote cooperation in the Division, ensuring that all units and sub-units function efficiently and effectively;
  • Coordinate with the Vice President for Academic Affairs and the Vice President for Research, Development, Extension and Production for the pursuit and realization of University objectives, programs, and activities;
  • Oversee the implementation of administrative issuances necessary for the efficient and effective administration of different units under the administrative and finance offices;
  • Supervise the preparation of periodic and annual budget estimates, proposals, and other required reports;
  • Recommend measures to improve schemes on ways and means of the University; and
  • Perform other functions as may be delegated by the University President.
