Vice President for Academic Affairs

The Vice-President for Academic Affairs is directly responsible to the University President for carrying out all educational policies, implementing programs and projects, and supervising curricular, instructional, and academic activities across the institution.

He/She shall perform the following duties and functions:

  • Plan and implement approved academic programs in coordination with division heads, deans, and campus directors.
  • Provide leadership in the Academic Council and ensure academic excellence across departments.
  • Implement university-wide academic policies and promote the development of faculty, staff, and students.
  • Supervise Campus Directors, Deans, Directors of Student Services, and all academic units.
  • Recommend personnel promotions and qualified individuals for positions under academic units.
  • Conduct meetings and conferences with campus and academic leaders.
  • Perform other academic functions as delegated by the University President.
