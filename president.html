THE PRESIDENT

University President

Dr. Nancy Talavera-Pascual

University President

The administration of the University shall be vested in the President who shall render full-time service. The powers and duties of the President of the University, in addition to those specifically provided for in RA 9157, shall be those usually pertaining to the office of the President of similar universities.

He/She shall also perform specific powers and duties as follows:

  • General supervision and control of administrative and financial operations of the SUC.
  • Head of all officers, faculty, and employees of the institution.
  • Implementer of general policies laid down by the Governing Board.
  • Prepares the agenda for GB, Administrative Council, and Academic Council meetings.
  • Presides over commencement exercises and other public functions.
  • Official communication channel between faculty/staff/students and the Governing Board.
  • Appoints competent personnel to fill vacancies as delegated by the GB.
  • May transfer personnel in accordance with existing laws, subject to GB confirmation.
  • Oversees discipline and academic standards across all departments.
  • Executes contracts and official documents on behalf of the University.
  • May invite scholars and experts to deliver lectures and authorize honoraria.
  • May designate an Officer-in-Charge during brief absences.
