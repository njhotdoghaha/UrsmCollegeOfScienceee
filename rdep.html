Vice President for Research Development Extension and Production

Dr. Marites M. Rio

Vice President for Research, Development, Extension & Production

The Vice-President for Research, Development, Extension, and Production is directly responsible to the University President on matters pertaining to research, extension, production services, non-formal education, training, and income-generating units.

In addition, he/she shall perform the following duties:

  • Plan and implement approved plans for research, development, extension, and production;
  • Implement university policies on RDEP programs and activities;
  • Supervise personnel under the division and promote their welfare;
  • Tap resources for research, development, extension, and production initiatives;
  • Supervise the preparation of research journals, newsletters, and reports;
  • Initiate linkages with government and non-government agencies;
  • Review and recommend research projects for funding;
  • Perform other functions as delegated by the University President.
